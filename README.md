# The Numbers Don’t Lie: Layoffs Analysis Since COVID-19

This repository accompanies my Medium article [*“The Numbers Don’t Lie: Which Country and Industry Are Seeing the Most Layoffs Since COVID-19?”*](https://aceyourgrace.medium.com/the-numbers-dont-lie-which-country-and-industry-are-seeing-the-most-layoffs-since-covid-19-ab0d70cefed9).  

It contains the data analysis and visualizations I created to explore global layoffs trends across industries and countries during and after the COVID-19 pandemic.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- **SAS**: Data cleaning, manipulation, and analysis of global layoffs datasets.  
- **Tableau**: Interactive dashboards and visualizations for insights presentation.  
- **Medium**: For publishing insights and story-driven analysis.

---

## Project Overview
The goal of this project was to answer:
- Which countries have experienced the most layoffs since COVID-19?  
- Which industries and company stages were most affected?  
- How do layoffs trends vary month by month since the pandemic started?

The project highlights include:
- Month-by-month layoffs trends across multiple countries.  
- Industry-wise breakdown of layoffs to identify sectors most impacted.  
- Analysis by company stage (startups vs established companies).  
- Interactive Tableau dashboard showcasing findings.

## Key Insights
- Consumer and retail industries saw the highest layoffs globally.  
- Startups and small companies experienced proportionally higher layoffs compared to large enterprises.  
- The peak of layoffs occurred in April 2020, with over 26,000 people affected in the datasets analyzed.
